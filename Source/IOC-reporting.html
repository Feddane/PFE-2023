what is an IOC ?

Indicators of Compromise (IOCs) are pieces of information that can help detect and identify potential security threats or attacks. IOCs can include any data that suggests malicious activity in IP addresses or domain names.

By collecting and analyzing IOCs reports, security professionals can identify patterns and trends in attacks and develop strategies to protect against future threats. these reports will be shared within the security community to improve threat intelligence and response efforts.

report an IOC

Reporting Indicators of Compromise (IOCs) to the appropriate authorities or security teams is crucial in the fight against cybercrime and other security threats. By sharing IOCs, security professionals can collaborate and coordinate efforts to identify, investigate, and respond to potential attacks.
