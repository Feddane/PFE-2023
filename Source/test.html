What is Cyber Incident Response (CIR) support?

Any organisation can use certified Cyber Incident Response (CIR) companies to help them deal with attacks.

You would use a company in the Cyber Incident Response (CIR) scheme if your organisation was the victim of a significant cyber attack. The NCSC-assured company would conduct all the Cyber Incident Response activities in relation to the attack to help your organisation recover.

Your organisation may also engage with a CIR company before any cyber security incident has taken place as part of your business continuity planning.
